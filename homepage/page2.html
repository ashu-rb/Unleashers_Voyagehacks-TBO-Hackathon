Trip to Andaman

  • Day 1:
    • Morning: Arrive at Port Blair Airport (IXZ) & Check in to the upscale Munjoh Ocean Resort.
    • Afternoon: Visit the Cellular Jail National Memorial, a historical prison that has now been converted into a museum.
    • Evening: Indulge in a rejuvenating spa treatment at the Munjoh Ocean Resort's wellness center.Take a sunset cruise to experience the breathtaking views of the Andaman Sea.
  • Day 2:
    • Morning: Engage in snorkeling or scuba diving to explore the vibrant underwater world and marine life.
    • Afternoon: Enjoy a delightful lunch at the Lighthouse Restaurant, serving delectable seafood dishes with panoramic views of the sea.
    • Evening: Witness the sunset at the Chidiya Tapu, a picturesque beach known for its scenic beauty. Relish a dinner at Barefoot Bar & Restaurant.
  • Day 3:
    • Morning: Check in to the luxurious Havelock Island Beach Resort, famous for its private beach and outdoor infinity pool.
    • Afternoon: Explore the Radhanagar Beach, consistently ranked among Asia's best beaches, and indulge in sunbathing or swimming.
    • Evening: Take a leisurely walk along the serene beach at sunset.Indulge in a rejuvenating massage at the Havelock Island Beach Resort's spa.
  • Day 4:
    • Morning: Visit the Bharatpur Beach, famous for its shallow waters and colorful coral formations
    • Afternoon: Visit the Laxmanpur Beach, known for its white sand and turquoise waters, and enjoy swimming or sunbathing.
    • Evening: Enjoy a memorable dinner at the Island View Restaurant, known for its authentic Indian cuisine and live cultural performances.
  • Day 5:
    • Morning: Check out and Check in to the Sinclairs Bayview, a luxury hotel known for its prime location and modern amenities.
    • Afternoon: Visit the Anthropological Museum, showcasing the rich cultural heritage of the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.
    • Evening: Take a leisurely stroll along the Marina Park, offering picturesque views of the harbor.
