Trip to Maldives

  • Day 1:
    • Morning: Arrive at Airport and take a speedboat/seaplane to your resort in the North Malé Atoll.Check in to your Overwater Bungalow.
    • Afternoon: Indulge in a rejuvenating spa treatment at the resort's wellness center.Take a leisurely walk along the pristine beach, immersing yourself in nature's tranquility.
    • Evening: Savor a delightful Indian dinner at the resort's signature restaurant, tantalizing your taste buds with authentic flavors.
  • Day 2:
    • Morning: Embark on a snorkeling excursion to explore the vibrant underwater world of the Maldives and encounter mesmerizing marine life.
    • Afternoon: Visit a local island and engage with the friendly Maldivian community.Learn about their unique culture and traditions.
    • Evening: Relish a romantic sunset dinner on a secluded beach, creating lasting memories under the starlit sky.
  • Day 3:
    • Morning: Try your hand at various water sports, such as kayaking, windsurfing, or jet skiing, adding an adrenaline rush to your vacation.
    • Afternoon: Embark on a dolphin cruise, setting sail in search of these playful creatures.Capture the magical moment of dolphins frolicking in their natural habitat.
    • Evening: Treat yourself to a Teppanyaki dinner, witnessing the culinary artistry of skilled chefs as they prepare your meal right before your eyes.
  • Day 4:
    • Morning: Visit the capital city, Malé, and explore its bustling markets and historical landmarks.Immerse yourself in the local culture and traditions.
    • Afternoon: Take a cooking class and learn to prepare traditional Maldivian dishes, unlocking the secrets of their unique flavors.
    • Evening: Indulge in a traditional Maldivian dinner at a local restaurant, savoring the authentic taste of the islands.
  • Day 5:
    • Morning: Enjoy a leisurely breakfast and take a final stroll along the beach, bidding farewell to the paradise you've come to cherish.
    • Afternoon: Check out of your resort and depart from Velana International Airport, carrying with you cherished memories of your Maldivian retreat.
    • Evening: Reflect on the transformative experiences and rejuvenating moments you encountered during your journey.
