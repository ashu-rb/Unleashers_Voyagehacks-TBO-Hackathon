Trip to Goa

  • Day 1:
    • Morning: Check in to your resort, Zuri White Sands Resort, Goa. Relax and unwind by the pool or on the beach.
    • Afternoon: Take a leisurely walk along the beach, enjoying the sunset and the cool evening breeze.
    • Evening: Dine at The Verandah, a fine-dining restaurant offering a panoramic view of the sea.
  • Day 2:
    • Morning: Indulge in an Ayurvedic spa treatment at one of the many spas in Goa, such as Angsana Spa & Salon at Taj Fort Aguada Resort & Spa.
    • Afternoon: Visit the Old Goa churches, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. These churches are a beautiful example of Portuguese-Indian architecture.
    • Evening: Enjoy a traditional Goan dinner at a local restaurant, such as O Pescador.
  • Day 3:
    • Morning: Take a boat trip to Grand Island, a small island off the coast of Goa. Snorkel or dive in the clear waters and see the colorful marine life.
    • Afternoon: Relax on the beach or go for a hike on one of the island's trails.
    • Evening: Return to Goa and dine at a beach shack, such as Curlies.
  • Day 4:
    • Morning: Visit the Dudhsagar Falls, one of the highest waterfalls in India. The falls are located in the Mollem National Park, which is home to a variety of flora and fauna.
    • Afternoon: Go for a jeep safari in the Mollem National Park. Spot animals such as tigers, leopards, and elephants.
    • Evening: Dine at a jungle lodge, such as The Western Ghats Lodge.
  • Day 5:
    • Morning: Relax and enjoy your final day in Goa. Go for a swim in the pool or on the beach.
    • Afternoon: Take a cooking class and learn how to make some traditional Goan dishes.
    • Evening: Dine at a restaurant with live music, such as The Fisherman's Wharf.
